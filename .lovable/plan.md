
## Nelson Waldorf School Class Exchange Fundraiser

### Overview
A warm, community-focused fundraising website for the Grade 8/9 class trip to a northern Saskatchewan indigenous community. The site will serve as both a public fundraising hub and an administrative tool, designed to be reusable as a template for future Waldorf class trips.

---

### 🏠 Public Pages

**1. Home / Landing Page**
- Welcoming hero section with the class mission and trip purpose
- Large, animated progress thermometer showing current amount vs. $10,000 goal
- Quick access buttons to donate, buy raffle tickets, and sign up for updates
- Brief story about the exchange program and what it means for both communities
- Placeholder for the indigenous community name (to be updated later)

**2. About the Trip**
- Detailed explanation of the exchange program
- What students will experience in the northern community
- What the visiting students will experience in Nelson
- Why this matters for both groups of young people

**3. Ways to Support**
- **Raffle Tickets**: Tiered pricing (e.g., 1 for $5, 5 for $20, 12 for $40) with online purchase
- **General Donations**: One-time donations of any amount
- **Upcoming Events**: Candy gram deliveries, can drives, grocery card sales (info only, managed offline)

**4. Donor Wall**
- Scrolling display of supporter names and optional messages
- Option for donors to remain anonymous
- Grouped by giving level or date

**5. Blog / Updates**
- Photo-rich posts from students about preparation, the trip itself, and hosting visitors
- Chronological timeline of the journey
- Comment-free for simplicity (updates only)

**6. Newsletter Sign-up**
- Simple email capture form
- Confirmation of subscription

---

### 🔐 Admin Dashboard (Login Required)

**Access Levels:**
- **Teachers (Full Admin)**: All features, user management, settings
- **Student Editors**: Can write and publish blog posts with photos

**Admin Features:**

**1. Donation Management**
- View all online donations and raffle purchases
- Add cash donations from in-person events (can drives, candy grams, etc.)
- Edit/update donation records
- Export donation data

**2. Raffle Management**
- View all ticket purchases
- Manage tiered pricing options
- Automated email confirmation with unique raffle entry numbers to purchasers

**3. Mailing List Management**
- View all subscribers
- Compose and send custom email campaigns
- Automatic blog notification emails when new posts are published
- Unsubscribe handling

**4. Blog Post Management**
- Create/edit/delete posts with photo uploads
- Publish or save as draft
- Schedule posts for future publication

**5. Dashboard Overview**
- Total raised with breakdown by source (raffle, donations, cash)
- Class progress as a whole (simple leaderboard showing total)
- Recent activity feed
- Subscriber count

---

### 💰 Payment & Email Integration

**Payments (Stripe)**
- Secure online payments for raffle tickets and donations
- Automatic receipts sent to donors
- Payment tracking in admin dashboard

**Email (Newsletter System)**
- Subscriber management with sign-up confirmation
- Blog notification emails (automatic when posts are published)
- Custom email campaigns for announcements
- Raffle ticket confirmation emails with entry numbers

---

### 🎨 Design Direction

**Warm & Community-Focused:**
- Earthy color palette (warm greens, terracotta, soft browns, cream)
- Friendly, approachable typography
- Photos and imagery celebrating connection, nature, and community
- Emphasis on the human story and the students' journey
- Mobile-responsive for easy sharing and donations on any device

---

### 🔄 Reusability for Future Classes

The site will be built with templating in mind:
- Easy-to-update goal amounts, dates, and destination
- Modular sections that can be customized
- Clear documentation for handoff to future classes
- All class-specific content (community name, dates, photos) easily replaceable

---

### Technical Foundation
- **Backend**: Lovable Cloud for database, authentication, and storage
- **Payments**: Stripe integration for secure transactions
- **Email**: Automated email system for newsletters and confirmations
- **Storage**: Photo uploads for blog posts stored securely
